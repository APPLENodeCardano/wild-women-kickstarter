The Vision

A Place Built
By & For Women

Deep in the hills of Southern Oregon, surrounded by 50,000 acres of untouched BLM wilderness, 160 acres is ready to become something women have never had before — a permanent, shared retreat center that belongs to the women who build it.

This isn't a spa. It's not a resort. It's a working wilderness home where women gather to heal, create, transition, and lead. Multiple facilitators and organizations host here year-round.

Your Indiegogo funding covers everything needed to get retreats on the land: building permits, well drilling, septic systems, and camp infrastructure. And then women begin building the cabins themselves.

The Signature Experience

Every Retreat
Builds a Cabin.

Here's what makes this retreat center unlike anything that exists: every woman who attends helps hand-craft a timberframe cabin that will shelter future retreats for generations.

You don't need experience. Expert timber framers are on-site to guide the work. You'll be taught how to shape and work the wood — and the cabin pieces are fitted together by the builders as a group. The cabin is always in progress during your stay, and you can work on it whenever you feel called to, between sessions, at sunrise, or late in the afternoon with a cup of tea and the Oregon hills around you.

By the time the center is fully built, it will have been raised entirely by the hands of women who came here to heal, create, and grow. Every beam, every joint, every cabin — a piece of that story.

🪵   Each cabin becomes a permanent part of the center

Questions

What You Want to Know

When does the retreat center open?

The retreat center is expected to open Summer 2027. All scheduling and reservations will be confirmed within 3 months of the Indiegogo campaign closing. Infrastructure — well, septic, permits, camp setup — will be completed first. Founding backers get first access and priority notification.

Do I need any building experience for the cabin work?

None at all. Expert timber framers are on-site to teach you how to shape and work the wood. You can come and go from the build freely throughout your retreat — there's no required schedule, just an open invitation to create something lasting.

Can I buy multiple $500 attendance tickets?

Yes — use the ticket quantity selector on the Attend a Retreat tier to reserve seats for friends, family, or your group. Each $500 covers one person's full four-day all-inclusive stay: meals, housing, programming, and cabin build access.

What does the $5,000 Cabin Sponsorship include?

You sponsor the building of one complete timberframe cabin with a $50 pre-launch deposit (toward the full $5,000). Your name or a personal dedication is engraved on a permanent plaque inside the cabin, you can reserve that cabin for any retreat you attend, and one complimentary retreat attendance is included. Your deposit is applied as a sponsorship credit toward the full $5,000.

What's included in the $10,000 Host Your Own Week?

Everything: exclusive use of the entire property for a full seven days, all meals and housing for up to 20 participants, a dedicated on-site coordinator, access to the cabin build throughout the week, an optional founder entrepreneurship session, and founding host recognition. You bring the vision — we handle the rest.

How does the $1 vote reserve my place in line?

Indiegogo timestamps every pledge. The moment you pledge, your position in the waitlist for that retreat is locked. First in, first seated for the launch — your dollar is your placeholder, your vote, and your voice.
